About Dipti Lipaan Art

Welcome to Dipti Lipaan Art, where tradition meets creativity. Our passion lies in preserving and promoting the beautiful art of Lipaan - a traditional form of wall and floor decoration that has been passed down through generations.

Each piece is crafted with love, dedication, and respect for our cultural heritage. We combine traditional techniques with contemporary aesthetics to create stunning artwork that resonates with both tradition and modernity.

Whether you're looking for custom artwork, learning opportunities, or simply want to appreciate the beauty of traditional art forms, you've come to the right place.
